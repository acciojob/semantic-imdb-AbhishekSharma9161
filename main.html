Summary

Inception is a mind-bending science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from deep within the subconscious during dreams. Cobb is offered a chance at redemption if he can accomplish the impossible—planting an idea into someone's mind, a process known as “inception.”
